Unemployment rates by State in April 2012

2. Description

It shows the recent unemployment rates by States in April 2012. A separate blue palette is used to distinguish states of different unemployment rates. The deeper the color, the higher the unemployment rate. You can see the detailed value when you put the mouse over the map. North Dakota,Nebraska and South Dakota have the lowest unemployment rates.
